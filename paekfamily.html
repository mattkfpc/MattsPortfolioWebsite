Family Website Wireframe

Link to the wireframe

What: A website dedicated to my family and its history.

Roles: Researcher, UI/UX Designer, Usability testing, Translator

I saw the value of family so much more clearly after being away from my family for 4 years. My father approached me about this project since my grandparents aren't getting any younger, and I immediately volunteered to lead and direct the rest of our "family team" to make a website to preserve the history of our family. The roles included: brother as back-end developer, sister as front-end developer, and a cousin as digital media consultant.

Research

This website had to be simple enough to be navigated by family members who had little to no experience with computers, so it was designed for simplicity and ease of use.

As the only bilingual member of the group, I also had to do extensive interviews, including recording and translating work.

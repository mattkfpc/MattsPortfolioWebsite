Muscle Game App

What: A web application that gamifies weightlifting by using a formula to calculate the user's level.

Roles: Usability testing, Researcher, UI Designer

Lifestyle gamification seemed to be an untapped gem in the app market, so this project was created to try to tap into a niche field: Exercise Gamification for Weightlifters. This app would ensure the user stays accountable and sets goals for weightlifting. Below are samples/descriptions from different parts of the design experience.

Research

To better understand a potential user, I interviewed 5 people from different backgrounds about role apps play in their exercise and ways that an app like Muscle Game could help them meet their goals.

Extensive research was done to come up with a formula that could be used to accurately calculate a person's level. The weightlifting community has many formulas that they use to measure their levels of success, so I had to ensure the forms reflected the data required by the formulas.

Wireframes (Balsamiq)

A UI/UX Developer constructed a high fidelity wireframe of the home page.

Usability Testing

Balsamiq has a feature that allows users to interact with the content, so I used this feature to test volunteers. Through the test, I was able to verify what worked and what didn't work, and I also had a better understanding of what the user is looking for in an application like this.

Front-End Development

The project was put on hold until further notice.

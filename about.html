Hello, I'm Matt

I'm a weight-lifting, adventurer seeking, book loving, web developer who loves programming and learning new things.

I wasn't always trying to get into the code. I dabbled in experiments, handled chemicals, and experimented with psychology throughout my college career while trying to prepare for pharmacy school. Ultimately, it wasn't for me.

In the meanwhile, I pursued another passion and taught students as an English teacher for a couple of years in South Korea.

I spent years traveling around the country side and I knew I wanted to do something, but I couldn’t grasp what I wanted until I stumbled upon a code camp I found online. Without much thought, I dove into HTML and CSS until I realized I could do this for hours and get sucked up and absorbed into my work. I eventually created side projects and worked on them continuously. I found myself thinking about potential projects and the languages that I had to learn to complete them. The abstract became concrete. I could finally call something my own.

While I’m not coding, I try to stick to my daily weight lifting schedule and read as many books as possible. I also enjoy jogging, cycling, and making my weekly meals.
